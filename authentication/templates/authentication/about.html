Online Food Delivery Web Application



For Customers:

    User-Friendly Interface:
      Home Page: Browse a curated list of local restaurants and popular dishes. Use search filters to find specific cuisines, dietary options, or restaurant ratings.
      Restaurant Pages: View detailed menus, photos of dishes, and customer reviews. Check out special offers and promotions.
      Order Tracking: Real-time updates on order status from preparation to delivery. See the estimated delivery time and track the delivery driver on a map.

    Seamless Ordering Experience:
      Easy Navigation: Add items to the cart with a single click and customize orders according to your preferences.
      Multiple Payment Options: Pay via credit/debit cards, digital wallets, or cash on delivery. Secure payment gateways ensure your transactions are safe.
      Personalized Recommendations: Get suggestions based on your previous orders, browsing history, and popular choices.

    Account Management:
      Profile Management: Update personal information, delivery addresses, and payment methods.
      Order History: View past orders, re-order your favorite meals, and keep track of your spending.
      Loyalty Programs: Earn points or discounts with frequent orders and special promotions.


For Sellers (Restaurants):

    Restaurant Dashboard:
      Order Management: View and manage incoming orders in real-time. Update order statuses and communicate with customers if necessary.
      Menu Management: Easily add, update, or remove menu items. Set prices, add descriptions, and upload images of dishes.
      Analytics: Access detailed reports on sales, order trends, and customer feedback to make data-driven decisions.

    Promotional Tools:
      Special Offers: Create and manage promotions, discounts, and limited-time offers to attract customers.
      Advertising: Promote your restaurant through featured listings and sponsored ads on the platform.

    Customer Engagement:
      Reviews and Feedback: Respond to customer reviews and feedback to improve service and build a positive reputation.
      Customer Insights: Gain insights into customer preferences and behavior to tailor your offerings and marketing strategies.

    Support and Integration:
      Customer Support: Access dedicated support for any technical issues or questions.
      Integration: Connect with POS systems, inventory management tools, and delivery logistics to streamline operations.
